Frozen Dihydrogen Monoxide

A Cool Solution

We all love our liquids to stay cool in the hot summertime, as such I am making this recipe to teach you how to freeze dihydrogen monoxide and use it to keep liquids at a reasonable temperature in the hot weather

Ingredients

  • 2 cups approx. Liquid Dihydrogen Monoxide

Steps

  1. Take ice cube trays to your sink and fill them with cold dihydrogen monoxide liquid.
  2. Place the dihydrogen monoxide trays in your freezer.
  3. Shut the door to your freezer.
  4. Allow around 4-6 hours for the dihydrogen monoxide to freeze and enter a solid state.
  5. (Optional) Feel free to experiment with other liquids like fruit juices etc.
